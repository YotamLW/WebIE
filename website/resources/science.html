SI units (International Standard)

Units give values practical context. The 6 SI base units are listed below. All other SI units can be constructed using the base units. All other units can be written as SI units multiplied by a scale (conversion) factor. Powers of units are different units, for example area (a power of length) cannot be converted into units of length.

  • Length - Metre [m]
  • Mass - Kilogram [kg]
  • Time - Second [s]
  • Electrical Current - Ampere [A]
  • Temperature - Kelvin [K]
  • Luminous Intenstity - Candela [cd]
